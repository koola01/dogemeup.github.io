Step-by-step guide in Buying DMU from PC

Safe Altcoin to the Moon!, Altcoin to DogeCoin!
Scroll Down

Step 1: Download Metamask

Download Metamask and create a wallet. Keep your phrase a secret! Never share it with anyone and store it properly!

Click “Get Chrome Extension” to install Metamask

Click “Add to Chrome” in the upper right

Click “Add Extension” to complete the installation

You will know Metamask has been installed when you see the fox logo on the upper right hand corner of your browser

Go to Metamask

Step 2: Connect Metamask to Binance Smart Chain

We want to access the Settings to point the wallet towards Binance Smart Chain nodes

Select Settings from the dropdown menu

On the Settings page, locate the Networks menu

Click Add Network in the top-right corner to manually add the Binance Smart Chain

Fill in the parameters

Network Name: Smart Chain

New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/

ChainID: 56

Symbol: BNB

Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com

Step 3: Add DogeMeUp to the Wallet

Tap the icon of Metamask at the upper right corner and tap "Add Token"

Copy the contract address and its parameters

Fill in the parameters

Contract Address

0xcd3e663819c6a2f64bb4b4c7f1acbdb3f12671b8

Token Symbol: DMU

Decimals of Precision: 18

Step 4: Buy BNB or other coins from any crypto exchanges like binance.com

Step 5: Swap BNB for DogeMeUp!

Go to Bakery Swap and pan over to Trade and click exchange

Go to BakerySwap

Pan over to "From" section and choose your available token

Pan over to "To" section and click "Select a Token" then paste the contract address of DMU then add it!

Fill in the parameters

Contract Address:

0xcd3e663819c6a2f64bb4b4c7f1acbdb3f12671b8

Type in how much of your existing coin would you trade for DMU and click "Swap"

Once the transaction has been cleared, you should have DogeMeUp in your Metamask wallet! Now all that's left is to HODL your DMU to the moon!
